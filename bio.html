About Thomas

Thomas began his vocal training at the age of 9 under the guiding hand of Margaret J. Davis, Suirvale School of Music. He then progressed through the ABRSM singing examinations with distinction. He has won numerous prizes at various feiseanna, including Feis Maitiu in Cork and Feis Ceoil in Dublin. His main interests are musical theatre and classical music. However, he does perform material from many genres. Thomas has performed in concert with Cara O'Sullivan (soprano), Jennifer Davis (soprano), Derek Ryan (bass), Affiniti (classical crossover group) and many other artists. He has played leading roles in musical theatre productions such as Les Miserables, Phantom of the Opera and Jesus Christ Superstar.
